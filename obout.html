    ABOUT SCHOOL

    MESSAGE FROM CORRESPONDENT

    “Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world”

    - Nelson Mandela.

    St.Antony’s Public School empowers the students to exhibit their hidden talents and facilitate the young minds with the apt perspective of life and shape them to become the future pillars of our nation.

    Upholding our Motto “Anchored in Excellence” we form our students by providing holistic education in academic as well as inculcating a strong sense of social, moral and emotional responsibility among our students. Here our teachers adopt excellent instructional methods and creative pedagogy to build a great learning environment that enables the students to acquire a strong foundation. We also create a conduit for exposure to learning and develop critical thinking skills, reasoning skills, problem solving skills, communicative skills, peer understanding and collaborative team spirit among students.

    We provide a healthy and hygienic learning environment and we have a strong efficient and talented faculty to facilitate our students’ all round development in the campus. Our students are provided with a happy and joyful learning experience which is our strength. The 21st learning skills are given priority and students are happy to have a system of integrated learning in the classrooms. As the New Education Policy 2020 aims at transforming the academic structure, we focus on technology accelerated learning in real classroom to empower our students in social and life skills crowned with values and ethics.
